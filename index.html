Hello there!

I'm Soham Chowdhury, a student of mathematics learning to coherently speak algebraic number theory and (eventually) arithmetic geometry.

I'm also a functional programmer interested in programming-language theory and expressive type systems, a self-taught guitarist and musician interested in counterpoint and functional harmony; a fan of a lot of music and writing; and an enthusiast of unexplained analogies and the endless unfamiliar.

This is my personal site, named after the Béla Bartók work.
